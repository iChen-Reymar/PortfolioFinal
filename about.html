Happiness arises when you help others. Those who continuously serve others are pleased and have no problems in their life. Research shows that they are less stressed and have better mental health. The aim of life is to benefit others. However, you should not expect anything in return; your objective should be to alleviate other people's suffering. "Service to others should be sincere and done from the heart; only then is it fruitful." Do any trees consume their own fruit? No. This tells us that humans should utilize their minds, bodies, and voice to assist others as well as themselves. When you do this, nature will repay you.
